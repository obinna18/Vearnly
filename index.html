About Me

Design with purpose,

I'm a 300-level Computer Science student at FUPRE (Federal University of Petroleum Resources) in Delta State, and the Chapter Lead for Data Scientists Network FUPRE. I design alongside developing — my background in product building means every visual decision I make is rooted in function, not just aesthetics.

Most of my design work has been for campus communities and independent businesses. These clients have zero tolerance for generic. That constraint has made me better.
